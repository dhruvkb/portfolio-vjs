Below are some of my projects
LecTut for Android - Android
LecTut is the Intranet study portal of IIT Roorkee. My project was to build an Android app to provide a native experience to the users and make it easy for them to manage the course material on the mobile phones.
 
Nobel - Node.js, Express and Socket.IO
Nobel makes it easy for IMG members to ping the lab and know who is present there. It allows IMG members to play a ring on a computer in the lab and then opens a chat between the caller and the lab occupants.
 
Amplio - Django
Amplio is an app for collecting reviews and feedback and then debating upon that feedback in an infinitely layered Reddit-like discussion. It is written in Django and will hopefully be a part of Channel i, the Intranet portal of IIT Roorkee.
